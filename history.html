15+ Years of Excellence

A Journey of Growth, Trust, and Innovation

Radha Madhav Transport Company has been at the forefront of the logistics industry in Uttar Pradesh for over a decade and a half. Our journey began with a simple vision - to provide reliable, safe, and efficient transport solutions to businesses and individuals across the state.

Under the dedicated leadership of our proprietor, Rekha JN Dwivedi, we have grown from a small local transport service to a comprehensive logistics network, earning the trust of hundreds of satisfied customers along the way.

Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones in our company's growth and development

2009

Company Foundation

Radha Madhav Transport Company was established by Rekha JN Dwivedi with a single truck and a dream to serve the transport needs of Uttar Pradesh. Started operations with local parcel delivery services in Valsad and surrounding areas.

2011

Fleet Expansion

Acquired our first 5 containers and expanded services to cover major cities including Kanpur and Lucknow. Established our first regional office to better serve our growing customer base.

2013

Network Expansion

Extended our service network to cover Varanasi, Gorakhpur, and Allahabad. Introduced daily parcel services connecting all major UP cities. Reached the milestone of 100 regular customers.

2015

GST Registration

Obtained GST registration (GSTIN: 24APXPD7268R1ZT) and implemented modern accounting systems. This marked our commitment to transparency and regulatory compliance.

2017

Multi-Location Operations

Opened our second major office in Thane, Maharashtra, expanding our operational footprint. This strategic move allowed us to serve a broader geographical area and improve service efficiency.

2019

Technology Integration

Implemented GPS tracking systems across our entire fleet, enabling real-time shipment tracking for customers. Introduced digital documentation and automated customer notifications.

2021

Excellence Recognition

Achieved 100% customer satisfaction rating and crossed 500 regular customers. Recognized as one of the most reliable transport companies in the region by industry peers.

2023

Fleet Modernization

Expanded our fleet to 20+ modern containers with enhanced safety features and improved fuel efficiency. Introduced specialized vehicles for different types of cargo.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched our professional website and digital booking system. Currently developing a mobile app for enhanced customer experience and planning expansion to pan-India operations.
